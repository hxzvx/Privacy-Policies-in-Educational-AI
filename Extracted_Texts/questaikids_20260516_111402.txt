Privacy Policy
Effective Date: December 2024
Last Updated: December 2024

1. Introduction
QuestAI Kids ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of children and their families. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our educational platform.

As a children's educational platform, we comply with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act), GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation), and Nigerian Data Protection Regulation (NDPR).

2. Information We Collect
From Children (Under 13):
First name only (no last names)
Age range (not specific birthdate)
Learning progress and achievements
Creative content (stories, art) - only stored locally unless shared with parent permission

From Parents/Guardians:
Email address for account creation
Payment information (processed securely through Paystack/PayPal)
Communication preferences

3. How We Use Information
Provide personalized learning experiences
Track learning progress and achievements
Send progress reports to parents (with consent)
Improve our educational content and platform
Process donations and sponsorships
Communicate about our mission and impact

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure
We NEVER sell or rent personal information. We only share data in these limited circumstances:

With parent/guardian consent for sharing achievements
With educational partners (schools, teachers) as authorized by parents
With service providers who help us operate the platform (all bound by strict privacy agreements)
When required by law or to protect safety

5. Data Security
We implement industry-standard security measures including:

SSL encryption for all data transmission
Secure cloud storage with access controls
Regular security audits and updates
Limited access to personal information on a need-to-know basis

6. Parent Rights and Controls
Parents have the right to:

Review all information collected about their child
Request deletion of their child's account and data
Refuse permission for information collection
Modify privacy settings and communication preferences
Export their child's learning progress and creations

7. International Data Transfers
Our platform serves children across Africa and globally. Data may be processed in Nigeria, the European Union, or the United States by our trusted service providers, all of whom maintain equivalent privacy protections.
